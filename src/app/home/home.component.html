Free Lindy Hop and West Coast Swing Social!

Who: Anyone who wants to dance, with or without a partner!
What: Two classes and a social
Where: Mine Brooke Park
When: Sundays 1-4pm May 1st-Oct 30th
How much: 100% free, though tips are very appreciated. Renting space is not free, and all extra procedes will go to improving the community (free water, guest instructors, hiring a band)
Covid Policies: Masks/Vax not required. Come as you feel comfortable, and if you are sick, please stay home and reach out so you know what you missed.
Topic of the month (May be changed based on community preferences):
May- Intermediate WCS, Beginner Lindy
June- Intermediate Lindy, Beginner WCS
July- Intermediate WCS OR Beginner Carolina Shag, Beginner Lindy
August- Intermediate Lindy OR Beginner Collegiate Shag, Beginner WCS
September- Intermediate WCS OR Beginner Blues, Beginner Lindy
