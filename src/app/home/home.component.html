Free Lindy Hop and West Coast Swing Social!

Who: Anyone who wants to dance, with or without a partner!
What: Two classes and a social
Where: Mine Brooke Park
When: Sundays 1-4pm May 1st-Oct 30th
How much: 100% free, though tips are very appreciated. Renting space is not free, and all extra procedes will go to improving the community (free water, guest instructors, hiring a band)
Covid Policies: Masks/Vax not required. Come as you feel comfortable
